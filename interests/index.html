Akpevweoghene's Interests

Akpevweoghene is interested in compelling storytelling that changes lives and the societal acceptance of certain good practices. She spends her free time reading books from Nigerian and other foreign authors. Her favourite remains the Game of Thrones books by George R. R Martin. She loves the work of Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie and Fatima Bala. She is currently reading Bisi sknajanca. Her typical work-free day consists of spending the entire day in bed reading fiction and communication research publications.
